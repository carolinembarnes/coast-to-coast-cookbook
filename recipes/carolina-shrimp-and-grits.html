Carolina Shrimp & Grits

Experiment (Not Final) Main Dish Seafood Southern

Add your story, inspiration, or memories for this Southern classic! For example: A comforting Carolina favorite, this dish combines creamy grits with savory shrimp and bacon—a true taste of home and hospitality.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs shrimp
  • ½ tsp red pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 2 cups grits
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream (milk or non-dairy milk to substitute)
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 6 slices of bacon
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Sprinkle shrimp with red pepper and paprika, and set aside.
  2. Bring broth, cream, butter, and salt to a boil. Stir in the grits and reduce heat to medium. Cook, whisking 7 to 8 minutes. Stir in cheese, if using.
  3. Cook bacon in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat for 3 minutes or until crisp. Remove bacon from pan.
  4. While the pan is still hot, add garlic until fragrant (about 30 seconds). Add shrimp and worcestershire sauce to the pan until the shrimp turn pink. Add back bacon and remove from heat.
  5. Serve over grits.
