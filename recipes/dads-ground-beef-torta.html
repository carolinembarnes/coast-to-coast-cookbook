Dad’s Ground Beef Torta

Dinner Main Dish Final Family Favorite Beef

Add your story or inspiration here! For example: A quick and comforting family classic, this Filipino-style ground beef torta is a weeknight staple from Dad’s kitchen, full of savory flavor and nourishing veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and chopped
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 10oz bag of frozen vegetables (peas, carrots, green beans)
  • 2 cups brown rice, cooked

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a large pan, 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add chopped onion and garlic; cook until onions are soft and meat is cooked through. Add soy sauce and lemon juice, and simmer on low for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook vegetables and rice according to package directions. Combine and serve.
