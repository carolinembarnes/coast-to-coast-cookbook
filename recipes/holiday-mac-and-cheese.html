Holiday Mac & Cheese

Main Dish Final Vegetarian Comfort Food Holiday

Add your story or inspiration here! For example: A rich and creamy baked mac & cheese, perfect for family gatherings and holiday tables—guaranteed to be a crowd-pleaser every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow pasta
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup flour
  • 4 cup milk
  • 6 cup freshly shredded sharp and medium cheddar
  • ½ tablespoon (kosher) salt
  • ½ tablespoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup panko bread crumbs

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package directions.
  2. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Sprinkle in flour and whisk and cook 2-3 minutes. Add in salt and pepper.
  3. Slowly pour in 4 cups of milk, whisking until smooth and heating to a low boil until thickened. Do not stop whisking and cooking until thick. Remove from heat.
  4. Grease a 9x13" baking dish and add the hot pasta to the dish. Over the top sprinkle 6 cups of freshly shredded cheese.
  5. Pour the thickened cream sauce over the hot pasta and cheese and let it sit until the cheese melts. Stir everything together.
  6. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add in panko bread crumbs, stirring constantly 3-5 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs over the mac and cheese.
  8. Bake in a preheated 325°F (160°C) oven for 12-15 minutes.
