Instant Pot Bolognese

Main Dish Final Pasta Instant Pot Beef

Add your story or inspiration here! For example: This rich and hearty Bolognese comes together quickly in the Instant Pot, making it a weeknight favorite with the depth of flavor you’d expect from hours of simmering.

Ingredients

  • 4 oz center cut bacon, chopped
  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 2-3 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2-3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 lb ground beef
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 2 (28 oz) cans crushed tomatoes
  • 3 bay leaves
  • ½ cup half & half
  • Salt and fresh black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Press sauté on the Instant Pot, and sauté the bacon until crispy, 4-5 minutes. Add the butter, onion, celery, and carrots. Cook until soft, 6-8 minutes.
  2. Add the ground beef, season with salt and black pepper, and sauté until browned. Break the beef into smaller pieces with a wooden spoon as it cooks.
  3. Once browned, pour in the wine and cook until it reduces down, 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, bay leaves, salt, and fresh cracked black pepper. Cover and cook on high pressure for 15 minutes.
  5. Allow natural release for 10 minutes. Stir in the half & half and serve over your favorite pasta, zucchini noodles, or spaghetti squash.
