Mama’s Baked Shrimp

Experiment (Not Final) Main Dish Seafood Southern Family Favorite

My husband’s grandmother is a fantastic cook. She is the epitome of a Southern grandmother. For holidays, we gather at her house and help ourselves to two or sometimes even three helpings of her delicious food. She has three children and their spouses with six grandchildren with two spouses -- needless to say, it’s a full house. Before I married Ben, I fell in love with her shrimp, which the family catches themselves in South Carolina’s beautiful low country.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb shrimp
  • Salt seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Ritz crackers
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (205°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cover shrimp in salt seasoning and garlic powder.
  3. Place Ritz Crackers in a bag and crush with a roller. Dump shrimp in Ritz Cracker bag and coat shrimp in cracker crumbs.
  4. In a greased pan, place shrimp in a single layer. Cut butter into small pieces and scatter across the top.
  5. Bake until shrimp are pink and topping is golden and crisp. Serve hot.
