Watermelon "Poke" Salad

Experiment (Not Final) Salad Vegetarian Summer

A refreshing, playful twist on classic poke, this salad swaps in juicy watermelon for fish and layers in pickled veggies, bold dressings, and plenty of crunch.
(Add your story or occasion here!)

Ingredients

Poke-Style Watermelon

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 3 cups cubed watermelon

Pickled Radish & Carrot

  • 10 oz radish, preferably daikon, thinly sliced
  • 7 oz carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar (or rice vinegar)
  • Pinch of salt

Sriracha Mayo

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped

Ginger Ponzu Dressing

  • ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • ¼ cup rice wine vinegar
  • ⅛ cup prepared ponzu sauce
  • ⅛ cup honey
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped

Salad

  • 10 oz vegetable slaw mix
  • 6 oz curly kale, chopped
  • 6 oz baby spinach, chopped
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • (Optional) 1 cup carrots, matchstick
  • (Optional) 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup edamame
  • 1 avocado, chopped
  • 2 tbsp crispy fried onions
  • 1 tbsp roasted black sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Pickled Radish & Carrot: Combine the pickle brine ingredients in a saucepan and boil on medium heat until the sugar dissolves (3 to 4 minutes). Stir occasionally. Remove from heat and cool.
  2. Place the radishes and carrots into a sterile pickling jar and pour in the brine. Move the jar gently to coat all the veggies.
  3. Leave the jar at room temperature for 3 to 4 hours, then refrigerate. Chill pickles for 30 mins to 1 hour before serving.
  4. Poke-Style Watermelon: Whisk together soy sauce, vinegar, lime juice, sesame oil, and sugar in a large bowl. Add watermelon; toss to coat. Let stand for 5 minutes.
  5. Ginger Ponzu Dressing: Whisk together olive oil, rice vinegar, ponzu sauce, honey, ginger, and garlic in a small bowl.
  6. Sriracha Mayo: Whisk together mayonnaise, Sriracha, lemon juice, and garlic in a small bowl.
  7. Combine slaw, kale, and spinach to create the salad base. Toss with dressing, scallions, cucumber, carrots, bean sprouts, and edamame.
  8. Arrange avocado, pickled radish and carrot on top. Sprinkle with onions and sesame seeds. Drizzle spicy mayo on top. Add poke-style watermelon just before serving.
